Traffic Accidents Data

West Bengal, 2018

Where/how/when/why do most traffic accidents occur?

This set of visualizations describe each individual circumstances when traffic accidents occur. To understand the causes of accidents, we hope to carry out machine learning algorithms to determine the combination of variables that are most closely associated with different types of traffic accidents.
